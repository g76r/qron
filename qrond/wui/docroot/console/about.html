Qron: the free scheduler

Qron is an enterprise-grade scheduler with time-based and event-based triggers, dependencies between tasks, resources management, monitoring, rest api, web console, and even more features. And it's free software.

About Tools & Libraries

Qron uses, embeds or includes several other free software. The most proheminent ones being: Qt, Bootstrap, jQuery, AnchorJS and Graphviz.

To start and supervise tasks, qron can use (and then relies on) the following tools: OpenSSH, Docker and local Linux process control.

Qron uses, embeds or includes free icons from Font Awesome.
